02Parallax

Stacked layers drift at different speeds.

StringParallax turns scroll travel into a vertical transform applied directly to each layer. Different strengths build real depth — the module owns the transform, so the scene composes itself.

Depth.

03Progress

A stable 0→1 value you can drive anything with.

StringProgress maps an element's scroll travel to an eased, clamped number exposed as the --progress variable and a scoped event. Here it spins, scales and fills.

04Lerp

Catch the velocity of the scroll.

StringLerp exposes the signed scroll-lerp delta as --lerp. We feed it into a skew and translate so the word physically reacts to how fast you scroll.

+3DWebGL · Three.js

A real WebGL scene, driven by StringTune.

A Three.js wireframe icosahedron + particle field. Scroll progress drives its rotation, the StringLerp scroll-velocity channel displaces its vertices, and the global cursor event tilts the whole scene. Pure WebGL — pauses offscreen, halves point count on mobile, and renders a single static frame under prefers-reduced-motion.
